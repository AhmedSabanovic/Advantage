ADNAN MEDEDOVIC

Managing Partner

Since establishment of the Advantage, we have defined performance excellence, commitment to our clients’ success and continual development as core of our values. Our goal is to bring real results to our clients. With more than 20 years of international experience in Western Balkan, Europe, Africa, and the Middle East, Adnan has been advisor to senior managers in strategy consulting area. Through his career he gained strong international experience on ICT industry landscape and infrastructure, real estate and infrastructure investment projects, financial institutions, key market trends and strategic priorities with specialization in the shaping of our clients’ strategies and leading project implementation. Mr. Mededovic has completed has Executive MBA at IEDC Bled School of Management, Slovenia and Mechanical Engineering degree from University of Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina.
