STRATEGY CONSULTING

CEO's task is focused on how best to manage the company toward its goals, and how to determine where the company is headed. We support CEOs focus on the important topics, critical areas where a CEO can lead the firm towards value creation. We work with CEOs in setting their strategic direction, based on a look at the view of their future, the shape of their portfolio, their competitive strategy, and the priorities and roadmap needed to achieve an optimal future. We support CEOs manage the company best establishing the right goals, measures, and rewards, setting the right leadership and structure, enhancing decision-making skills, and developing the right talent. Our comprehensive approach and use of analytical thinking to develop corporate strategy enables our clients to clearly map their future steps and support them to get to position “where they want to be”. We help our clients identify their fundamental business objectives and implement measures to track their success. Using strategies that changes attitudes, values and the structure so client can adopt to new corporate strategies, technologies, market variables, challenges, etc. Our organizational development solutions help our clients increase ability to develop effective system. Our consultants in collaboration with client’s team implement change management program through structured approach: plan – design – execute. This approach is based on strong planning, bold leadership, excellent teams and project managers, commitment, engagement, and empowerment.

FINANCIAL ADVISORY

Corporate performance is increasingly tightly managed. Risks are growing. Creating value across all business areas is the main task of CFO. Our advisors support CFOs in their activities that leads to transformation of finance organization, shape portfolio strategies, undertake major investment and financing decisions. We support the CFO on two main topics:


strategy, and

value-based management. 


We work with client's team to determine which strategies and business models create the most value, to fund corporate growth and maximize long-term value, to explain corporate strategy clearly and manage performance expectations.
